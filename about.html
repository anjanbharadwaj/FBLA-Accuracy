Our History

Accuracy Inc. is steadfast on its journey to revolutionize financial accounting with its innovative approaches to ensuring customers receive the best possible experience. Our story began last year in 2018, when our founder and CEO, Dianna Whitter, noticed that although there were plenty of accounting firms based in the US, their time was spent solely catering to top tier enterprises and corporations, leaving out the millions of small businesses and individuals that struggle to manage their finances every year.

Equipped with financial and legal expertise, the firm set out to innovate and democratize accounting solutions by developing customizable plans for businesses and individuals of all sizes and financial positions. Our core philosophy is based on the fact that we don’t believe that we are service-providers for your organization - rather, we view ourselves as an extension of your organization, providing personalized support to ensure you maximize your potential and aren’t impeded by financial analysis.

Over the past year, we have remained true to our values and our commitment to high quality service, and continue to embody our original spirit of providing valuable financial accounting services to businesses that can thrive with our help. Accuracy is experienced with providing financial accounting, tax management, and legal services to our clients around the world. Serving over 200 organizations across the globe, our CPAs are well-versed with international accounting practices and have access to state-of-the-art resources to ensure that your needs are met. We have experience with tending to the financial affairs of all sorts of business structures, ranging from LLCs to S-Corporations, meaning your organization is in great hands.

Join us in our journey to revolutionize accounting and take your business to the next level.

How We Give Back

Accuracy is also proud to partner with KPMG to provide free-of-use accounting systems to local businesses in underdeveloped neighborhoods in metropolitan areas across the country, including Brooklyn, NYC, Detroit, MI, Oakland, CA, and more. By providing online financial support, Accuracy aims to help get these businesses on their feet and help them develop a steady stream of income by focusing on their operations, letting us handle their financial statements and consolidating their ledgers and numerous accounts into one simple portal.

Lastly, we also license out our novel accounting software at discounted rates to non-profit education organizations. Please reach out to our sponsorship director at philanthropy@accuracy.com for more information on how you can take part in this program to bring our state-of-the-art accounting software to your organization.

Here’s some of the communities that we’ve reached:

Fremont Union High School District (FUHSD)
Accuracy holds weekly educational meetings with students from schools in the Fremont Union High School District based near their headquarters in the sunny Silicon Valley, where Accuracy employees donate their time and effort to educate young minds about financial literacy, teaching them the importance of managing personal finance and providing advice on how they can begin to develop useful financial habits that will benefit them in future. Students are also taught the process of filing tax returns, handling debit and credit, and more basic accounting terminology that will help them as they kickstart their careers.

KPMG Work Shadow Program
Accuracy has partnered with KPMG’s Work Shadow Program to provide high school internships to students in underprivileged communities across Northern California. We believe it is vital that we educate future generations to ensure that they have the proper skills to handle their finances in the future - our philosophy is that there is no better way to do this than by involving students in our accounting and legal teams to give them first-hand experience. If you are a student who is interested in taking part in our program, please visit our “Opportunities” tab.
