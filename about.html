Our History

 Accuracy accounting has been revolutionizing the accounting field for the last 75 years. Formed shortly after the great depression, we were fed up with the corruption and greed of corporate America and strove to create an organization founded on loyalty, trust, and compassion for our members. With humble beginnings out of our college dorm room at Yale, we have always remained bold and audacious in our goals; we now have over 900 employees and 100 corporate partnerships with fortune 1000 companies. Yet as we grow, we never forget to stay true to our core values and remember to serve the needs of our clients. As we expand our services to include bookkeeping, taxes, and audits, we have been looked to magnify our social good; since inception, we have been donating 5% of profits to our alma mater, Yale, to give the gift of education to as many young minds as possible.

How We Give Back

 Accuracy is committed to helping adolescents and young adults - the next generation - learn about finance and ensure that they are prepared for their futures. Accuracy holds weekly educational meetings with students from schools in the Fremont Union High School District based near their headquarters in the sunny Silicon Valley, where Accuracy employees donate their time and effort to educate young minds about financial literacy, teaching them the importance of managing personal finance and providing advice on how they can begin to develop useful financial habits that will benefit them in future.
 Accuracy is also proud to partner with KPMG to provide free-of-use accounting systems to local businesses in underdeveloped neighborhoods in metropolitan areas across the country, including Brooklyn, NYC, Detroit, MI, Oakland, CA, and more. By providing online financial support, Accuracy aims to help get these businesses on their feet and help them develop a steady stream of income by focusing on their operations, letting us handle their financial statements and consolidating their ledgers and numerous accounts into one simple portal.
 Lastly, we also license out our novel accounting software at discounted rates to non-profit education organizations. Please reach out to our sponsorship director at philanthropy@accuracy.com for more information on how you can take part in this program to bring our state-of-the-art accounting software to your organization.
