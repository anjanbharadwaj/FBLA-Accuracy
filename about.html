Our History

 Accuracy accounting has been revolutionizing the accounting field for the last 75 years. Formed shortly after the great depression, we were fed up with the corruption and greed of corporate America and strove to create an organization founded on loyalty, trust, and compassion for our members. With humble beginnings out of our college dorm room at Yale, we have always remained bold and audacious in our goals; we now have over 900 employees and 100 corporate partnerships with fortune 1000 companies. Yet as we grow, we never forget to stay true to our core values and remember to serve the needs of our clients. As we expand our services to include bookkeeping, taxes, and audits, we have been looked to magnify our social good; since inception, we have been donating 5% of profits to our alma mater, Yale, to give the gift of education to as many young minds as possible.

Our Staff

 Accuracy aims at making the best hiring decisions possible. With a focus on customer service and integrity in everything we do - accuracy heavily screens applicants on not only skills in finance and accounting but also interpersonal skills required to successfully deal effectively with large companies.
